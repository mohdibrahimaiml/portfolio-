Focus: Scalable LLM architecture with Sparse MoE, FP8, memory efficiency, and hooks for symbolic safety (Z3/Lean4).

Grok-3+: Scalable, Safe, and Energy-Optimized Architecture

DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.15341810

Zenodo Record

Why it matters: Rewards good reasoning structure, not just final tokens.
